We've given our website a full rebuild, and you're looking at the result.

The goal was simple: make it faster and easier for contractors and specialty trade owners to find what they need — whether that's a straight answer about [pricing](/pricing/), a look at [how we're different](/why-we-are-different/), or a quick number from one of our free tools.

## What's here

- **Free calculators.** The [Labor Cost Calculator](/labor-calculator/) shows what an employee really costs per hour, and the [Profit Margin Calculator](/profit-margin-calculator/) tells you whether a job actually made money. Both now show results instantly.
- **The Toolbox.** Our [financial tools for contractors](/toolbox/) — planners, dashboards, and forecasts you can buy and use today.
- **Industry pages.** Whether you run a [construction](/construction-accountants/), [HVAC](/hvac-accounting/), [electrical](/accountants-for-electricians/), [plumbing](/accountants-for-plumbers/), or [solar](/accountants-for-solar-companies/) business, there's a page that speaks your language.

## What's next

We'll keep posting accounting updates, tips, and practical guides for the trades right here on the blog. If there's a topic you want us to cover, [tell us](/getting-started/) — we build this stuff for you.
